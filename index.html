Tools of choice

Every skill is supported by some toolset, especially in a digital era we live in. Those aren’t crucial for bringing expected results but they at least make our lifes easier.

My Tools Deck

From the wide variety of tools I’ve tested & used throughout my career, some became my favorites. These tools I rely on & highly recommend when working with clients.

G Suite

Assets & Docs

Yes, the power sits in Google suite. I use it to store my files, keep & share client’s assets, create docs, form sheets & surveys, as well as manage emails.

ClickUp

Project Management

Easy to use, collaborative, coherent. All I need to make my projects run smoothly. I couldn’t make my project management easier without ClickUp.

Adobe Illustrator

Visual Language Design

No matter what the end goal, when it comes to vector graphics, starting from icons through logo design, typography to full identities I do it all with AI.

Zapier

Automation

Business logic & automation make our lives easier by improving and speeding up processes. Zappier allows me to connect different digital tools to set your business’ growth on autopilot.

Framer

Prototyping

Framer helps me add “soul” to my prototypes. Smooth & intuitive interactions make the prototypes feel like a real products. Time for user testing? Framer is my answer.

Figma

UX/UI Design

My ultimate tool for digital products creation. From brainstorming (FigJam), through flows, wireframes, Hi-Fi user interfaces design to prototyping.

Webflow

No-Code Development

Modern, flex, responsive & most of all memorable websites. There’s no better tool for no/low-code development. I use Webflow on daily basis to turn visions into fully functional websites.
